✦ HUDDLE AI

Saturday looks busy — add one more barista, 19:00–23:00?

Huddle AI writes next week's schedule. You just approve it.

Huddle AI learns how your venue actually runs — the busy nights, the reliable openers, who always swaps out of Sundays — and drafts the full week for you. No dragging shifts around a grid.

◉

It reads every shift you've already run

Check-ins, late arrivals, overtime, swaps and no-shows all feed the model. There's nothing extra to log — the data comes from how your team already uses Huddle.

∿

It finds your rhythm

After a few weeks it knows Friday from 20:00 needs three people on the floor, Tuesday morning needs one, and that the first week of the month always runs hot.

☺

It knows your people

Availability, requested days off, contracted hours, who's still training and who's strongest on a closing shift — all of it is respected before a name lands on a shift.

✦

It builds the week without being asked

Every Sunday a full rota is already waiting: coverage balanced, hours spread fairly, no double-bookings. Approve it, tweak a shift, or publish it as it is.
